    Answers up front

    Questions we hear every week.

    Don't see your question? Drop it in the form above — a real Go2Roofing advisor (never a chatbot) will reply within an hour.

    How long does a roof replacement actually take?

    For a typical Ontario home (1,800–2,400 sq ft), our W-2 crews complete tear-off and installation in 1–3 days. Larger homes, steep pitches, or multi-layer tear-offs extend that to 4–5 days. You'll get a written day-by-day schedule before we start.

    What does Go2Roofing's warranty actually cover?

    Every system ships with a lifetime workmanship warranty (transferable once if you sell) and the manufacturer's material warranty (30, 50, or lifetime depending on tier). Workmanship issues are remediated at zero cost — including labour, materials, and any interior repairs caused by the original install.

    Do you offer financing?

    Yes — 0% APR for 12 months and up to 120-month extended plans through our financing partner. Soft credit pulls only, decisions in under 5 minutes. Ask your advisor for current promos.

    What happens if it rains during install?

    Our crews monitor radar in 15-minute increments. If weather closes in, we tarp and seal every exposed deck section before leaving the site. Your home is never left vulnerable overnight — guaranteed in writing.

    Can I just repair my roof instead of replacing it?

    Often, yes. If your roof is under 15 years and the damage is localized, a targeted repair is the right answer — and we'll tell you that even if it's a smaller invoice for us. Go2 advisors are salaried, not commissioned.

    Are you insured? Licensed?

    Fully. $5M liability insurance, WSIB clearance on file, and certified Master Elite (GAF), Shield Pro+ (IKO), and SELECT (CertainTeed) installer. We'll email you all three on request before signing anything.

    Do you handle insurance claims?

    Yes. Storm/hail/wind damage assessments include a full photo + thermal report formatted for adjuster review. We can also meet your adjuster on-site at no charge.
